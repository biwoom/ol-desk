# The Great Chronicles of the Buddhas by Mingun Sayādaw(Bhaddanta Vicittasārābhivaṁsa)

A revised edition of the translation of Mingun Sayādaw’s great work, detailing all that is known about the previous 24 Buddhas, our Buddha Gotama and his disciples.

translated by T. U Ko Lay, U Tin Lwin and U Tin Oo


revised and redited by Ānandajoti Bhikkhu 

Version 1.2, August 2024
